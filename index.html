How it Works

We will guide you through the process

Strategy Planning

Once you join WSDV, a veteran teammate will reach out to you to book your strategy session. You'll get a veteran coach that will be your facilitator from beginning to end. Your coach will strategize with you via one-on-one education to come up with the best plan to move forward.

Education

Our process will help review your existing medical evidence and identify gaps between your current VA disability benefits and what you actually qualify for by law. We will help you build the complete claims package...strategy, evidence, submission!

Medical Evidence

You'll have the option to connect with one of our independent medical providers to help strengthen your claim. These medical providers will be available to help you get that "nexus" requirement for service connection.

WE CAN HELP

WSDV is an education-based consulting company for disabled veterans looking to increase their VA disability benefits and who wish to learn more about the process. We also connect veterans with independent medical professionals in our referral network for medical examinations, disability evaluations, and credible Independent Medical Opinions & Nexus Statements for a wide range of disability conditions.
